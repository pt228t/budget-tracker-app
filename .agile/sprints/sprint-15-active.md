# Sprint 15: Advanced Analytics (Heatmap & Spend Split)

## Goal
Implement advanced analytics including a Day-of-Week spending heatmap (B-027) and a Person-wise spending split chart (B-028) in the Analytics view to provide deeper spending insights.

## Tasks
- [ ] **B-027: Day-of-week spending heatmap**
  - Aggregate transaction amounts and counts per day of the week (Monday - Sunday).
  - Render a visual representation (or color-coded grid/bars) showing day-of-week spending intensity on the Analytics page.
- [ ] **B-028: Person-wise spending split chart**
  - Aggregate transactions by the user who logged/paid the expense (`paid_by` column).
  - Render a comparison chart (pie/donut or split bar) in the Analytics view showing how total expenses are split between users.
